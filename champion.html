The Vision

Redefining What It Means to Be a Rocket

The Champions Complex is one of the most meaningful investments ever made in the lives of Toledo student-athletes. This project will renovate and re-imagine the existing Health Education Building, covering 74,000 square feet and maximizing its centralized campus location. Every design decision has been made with the daily experience of our Rockets in mind.

This facility will elevate the student-athlete experience, strengthen our competitive edge, and reinforce Toledo Athletics as a place where excellence is expected and supported. Bryan B. Blair, VP and Director of Athletics

What Is Inside

World-Class Spaces for World-Class Athletes

From training grounds to study halls, every space within the Champions Complex is purpose-built to support the complete student-athlete experience.

Indoor Turf Facility

A dedicated indoor training space that allows year-round practice for baseball, softball, and multi-sport use regardless of weather conditions.

Training Table

A modern dining facility designed to fuel peak performance, providing nutritionally balanced meals in a communal setting that fosters team culture.

Academic Center

Dedicated lounge and study spaces where student-athletes can focus on their academic goals with access to tutoring and academic support services.

Champions Entry

A grand entryway that sets the tone for the entire facility, celebrating Toledo's athletic legacy while inspiring the next generation of champions.

Baseball Team Suite

State-of-the-art team spaces featuring locker rooms, meeting areas, and lounge facilities designed to bring baseball back to the main campus.

Softball Team Suite

Purpose-built team facilities that give Rocket softball their own dedicated home on campus with locker rooms and a comfortable player lounge.

Champions Corridor

The iconic spine of the facility connecting every space, lined with the history of Rocket Athletics and built to inspire every athlete who walks through it.

The Impact

Transforming Lives, Building Champions

The Champions Complex will single-handedly improve the lives of all 450+ Rocket student-athletes while returning baseball and softball programs to the main campus from Scott Park.

  • Centralized academics, nutrition, health, wellness, and training under one roof
  • Returns baseball and softball to main campus, fostering greater team unity
  • Modern team meeting spaces with film review and presentation capabilities
  • Year-round indoor training facility for multi-sport use
  • State-of-the-art nutrition center supporting peak athletic performance
  • Dedicated academic support spaces with tutoring resources
